### Notes

- Quantifiers

 Large quanties: 
  - Uncountables:
         A lot of, much 
  - Countables:
	A lot of ,many

 Medium quantities
  - Countables: 
	Some, a numeber of 
  - Uncountables:
	Some, a certain/fair amount of
 Small quantities:
  - Countables:
	few
   - Uncountables:
	little

Examples:

   She had few moments on her own


   She saves a little money every month.


We use 'amount of' with uncountables nouns
       'number of' with coutnables nouns
